Who We Are

The NYU Concrete Canoe team is a student-led organization that designs, builds, and races concrete canoes. We compete annually in the ASCE Concrete Canoe Competition, showcasing our engineering skills, material science expertise, and teamwork.

Our team brings together students from diverse engineering disciplines, united by a passion for hands-on learning and collaborative innovation.

What We Do

CAD

Modelling the hull in CAD, iterating on shape and cross-section to balance speed, stability and the volume of concrete the design needs.

Structural Analysis

Working out how the canoe carries load, so the hull holds up under paddlers and transport without adding weight where it is not needed.

Finite Element Analysis

Simulating stress across the hull to find where it concentrates, then reinforcing those areas before anything is cast.

Look and Theme

Choosing the finish, colour and theme that give each canoe its identity, and carrying that through the stand, display and presentation.

Chrysalis

Our 2026 canoe represents renewal and transformation. Like a butterfly emerging from its chrysalis, Chrysalis pioneers a meshless fiber reinforced concrete design, pairing a carbon negative mix with recycled sustainable materials and an AI-powered paddle trainer.

She carried the team to 1st place overall at the ASCE Metropolitan Regionals and 3rd at Nationals. It is the best finish in program history.
