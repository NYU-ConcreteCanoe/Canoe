Who We Are

The NYU Concrete Canoe team is a student-led organization that designs, builds, and races concrete canoes. We compete annually in the ASCE Concrete Canoe Competition, showcasing our engineering skills, material science expertise, and teamwork.

Our team brings together students from diverse engineering disciplines, united by a passion for hands-on learning and collaborative innovation.

What We Do

Design

CAD modelling, structural and finite element analysis, and the look and feel of the canoe.

Build

Mixing the composite we designed, then building, refining and iterating over it.

Paddle

Training to get on the water for the slaloms and races.

Compete

Competing in the ASCE Concrete Canoe Competition.

Chrysalis

Our 2026 canoe placed 1st overall at Regionals and 3rd at Nationals, built on a meshless carbon negative design and the debut of our ML paddling trainer.
